Founding Story

ClassEdu is an innovative online learning platform that was founded in 2023 with the mission to transform education through technology. Built by passionate educators and developers, ClassEdu aims to make learning more accessible, engaging, and future-oriented for learners around the world.

We believe that education is the key to unlocking potential, and our platform is designed to support students, professionals, and institutions in achieving their goals.
